Module 1: Office Administration & Management

N6 Travel Office Procedures

Developed by Karabo Bopape

Unit 1: Advanced Office Administration

  • Office types, functions, and administrative structures at N6 level
  • Policies, procedures, compliance frameworks (including POPIA, FICA)
  • Management of records—paper and digital; archiving and retention policies

Unit 2: Office Equipment & Technology Enhancements

  • Advanced features of multifunction devices, digital dictation, conferencing tools
  • Security protocols: encryption, user access controls, backup procedures
  • Integration with booking systems (GDS, CRM, accounting software)

Unit 3: Financial Administration

  • Advanced payment processing: BSP, GDS settlements, reconciling accounts
  • Foreign exchange, travel card management, vendor billing and invoicing
  • Budget preparation, cost control, auditing and compliance checks

Unit 4: Human Resource Administration

  • Staffing: roles, responsibilities, recruitment
  • Training, performance appraisals, scheduling and rostering
  • Labour and health & safety legislation (OHSA, UIF, COIDA compliance)

Unit 5: Office Planning, Security & Disaster Management

  • Space optimization, ergonomics, asset protection and surveillance
  • Data protection, risk management, disaster backup planning
  • Emergency procedures, fire safety, evacuation, continuity planning

Unit 6: Legal and Regulatory Frameworks

  • POPIA, FICA, VAT, consumer protection legislation affecting travel offices
  • Contract law, client terms and conditions, supplier agreements
  • Liability considerations: travel insurance, indemnity, refund and dispute policies
