Module 1: Travel Services Management

Unit 1: Structure of the Travel Environment

  • Components of the industry: travel agencies, tour operators, wholesalers, facilitators.
  • Roles and interrelationships at senior level.
  • Corporate governance, stakeholder management, ethical frameworks.

Unit 2: Travel Agency Management

  • Management functions: planning, organising, leading and controlling.
  • Strategic planning: vision, mission, objectives, SWOT analysis.
  • Operational management: workflow, performance standards, KPIs.

Unit 3: Client Relations & Customer Care

  • Advanced service excellence: profiling, loyalty programmes, CRM systems.
  • Handling high-value clients, VIPs, sensitive cases.
  • Complaint resolution, recovery, and follow-up protocols.

Unit 4: Financial Management in Travel

  • BSP & GDS reconciliations, credit control, debtor management.
  • Revenue streams: commissions, service fees, mark-ups.
  • Budgeting, forecasting, financial audits and internal controls.

Unit 5: Human Resources & Professional Development

  • Staff development: training needs analysis, coaching, mentorship.
  • Performance management, appraisals, incentives.
  • Legal compliance: labour law, POPIA, FICA, workplace ethics.
