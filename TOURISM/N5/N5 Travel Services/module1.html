  • Factors affecting travel trends: economy, politics, technology, health, security.

Unit 2: Sustainable and Responsible Tourism

  • Definition and principles of sustainable tourism.
  • Responsible traveler behavior and community involvement.

Unit 3: Changing Consumer Behavior

  • Customization and experiential travel demand.
  • Shift towards self-service, digital bookings, and mobile apps.
  • Value for money and last-minute travel packages.

Unit 4: Technology and Innovation

  • Role of the internet, artificial intelligence, and virtual reality in travel.
  • Use of Global Distribution Systems (GDS) and travel management platforms.
  • Online reviews, influencer marketing, and social media trends.

Unit 5: Current Issues and Opportunities

  • Post-COVID-19 recovery and new safety protocols.
  • Travel bubbles, health passports, and digital vaccination records.
  • Career opportunities arising from trends in the travel industry.
