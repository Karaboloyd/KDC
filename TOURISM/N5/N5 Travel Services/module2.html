Module 2: Tour Planning and Costing

Part of the N5 Travel Services Study Pack

Developed by Karabo Bopape

Unit 1: The Tour Planning Process

  • Steps: Market research, destination selection, itinerary creation, costing, promotion.
  • Tour types: FIT (Free Independent Traveller), group tours, escorted tours.
  • Use of destination knowledge and supplier networks in planning.

Unit 2: Tour Itinerary Design

  • Structure of an itinerary: day-by-day activities, inclusions, exclusions.
  • Key elements: timings, distances, attractions, meal plans, accommodation.
  • Tailoring for different markets: leisure, business, cultural, eco-tourism.

Unit 3: Tour Costing Techniques

  • Fixed vs variable costs: accommodation, meals, entrance fees, guides, admin.
  • Markup and profit calculation methods.
  • Package pricing structures and seasonal adjustments.

Unit 4: Quotations and Proposals

  • Preparing clear and detailed quotations for clients.
  • Components: itinerary, pricing, validity period, payment terms.
  • Use of templates and software tools (Excel, Tourwriter, etc.).

Unit 5: Booking and Confirmation Procedures

  • Confirming services with suppliers (accommodation, transport, activities).
  • Issuing vouchers and final documents.
  • Client confirmation and pre-departure checks.
